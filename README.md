Recipe to make Maggi:
Required things:-
1. Maggi nnoodles
2. Masala (Spices)
3. Water
4. Veges of your choice
Procedure:-
1. Boil water
2. Put the  noodles in the boiling water
3. Add veges of your choice
4. Stir the noodles
5. After about 2-3 minutes , the Maggi is ready to serve .
6. Voila you have your Maggi ready!
